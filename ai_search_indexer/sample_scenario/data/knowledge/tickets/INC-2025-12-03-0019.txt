Root cause: Automated maintenance job launched VACUUM FULL on the events table (40GB) without the --analyze-only flag. VACUUM FULL takes an exclusive lock and rewrites the entire table, blocking all queries.

Resolution: Killed the VACUUM process. Updated maintenance script to use VACUUM ANALYZE (no exclusive lock). Added pg_stat_activity monitoring for long-running maintenance queries.

Affected services: All (database is shared dependency)
Duration: 14 minutes
Customer impact: Complete service outage. ~3,200 failed requests during window.
Post-mortem action items:
  1. Replace VACUUM FULL with VACUUM ANALYZE in all maintenance scripts
  2. Add circuit breaker: kill maintenance queries running > 5 minutes
  3. Schedule maintenance during lowest-traffic window (Tuesday 04:00 UTC)
